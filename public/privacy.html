TaskTHIS Privacy Policy

Elucidata is committed to protecting the privacy of those who visit the TaskTHIS.elucidata-apps.com website and use the TaskTHIS Service. Your continued use of this site following the posting of any amendment, modification, or change shall constitute your acceptance of the amendment. We will notify the primary TaskTHIS Service account holder by email when significant changes are made to the privacy policy.

We collect the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email, information about the pages consumers access on the TaskTHIS website, and information voluntarily submitted by the consumer (for example, filling out a survey or service registration form). The types of information that may be requested include your name and email address.

Your information will not be sold to or shared with other companies or organizations for commercial purposes.

Use of Cookies

When you use the TaskTHIS Service we may store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a “cookie” or similar file. Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on the TaskTHIS website. We do not use cookies to spy on you or otherwise invade your privacy. They cannot invade your hard drive and steal information.

We use cookies to help you navigate the TaskTHIS website and Service as easily as possible, and to remember information about your current session. You must enable cookies on your web browser to use the TaskTHIS Service.

Security and Storage

The TaskTHIS website and Service has industry standard security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. While there is no such thing as “perfect security” on the internet, we will take all reasonable steps to insure the safety of your information.

Additionally, you retain all rights of ownership to the data you have stored on the TaskTHIS Service. We will not sell or share this data with any third-parties or use this data to compete with you or advertise to your clients. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.
